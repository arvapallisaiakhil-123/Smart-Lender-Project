About Smart Lender

Smart Lender is an intelligent web application that predicts whether a loan applicant is eligible for approval using Machine Learning. The system analyzes customer information such as income, education, employment, credit history, loan amount, and property area to assist financial institutions in making faster and more accurate lending decisions.
